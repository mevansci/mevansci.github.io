Michael Evans

I'm a senior student and research assistant at Old Dominion University completing a B.S. in Computer Science. I am applying for Ph.D. programs to begin in Fall 2025.

At Old Dominion University I worked on a project involving large language models on the task of scientific claim verification, where I was advised by Dr. Jian Wu as part of the WS-DL research group.

Previously I worked on a joint research project between Lawrence Tech and Michigan State University on developing self-drive algorithms for electric vehicles as part of the CS & AI Robotics Lab. Co-advised by Dr. CJ Chung and Dr. Josh Siegel.
